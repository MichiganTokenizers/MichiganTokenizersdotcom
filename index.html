Who We Are

Michigan Tokenizers is a data and technology consultancy built for small and medium-sized businesses. We combine deep data infrastructure expertise with hands-on experience across multiple blockchain ecosystems — XRP Ledger, Flare Network, Cardano, and Midnight — and practical AI development to deliver systems that actually ship and operate in the real world. We work at the intersection of what's possible and what's worth building.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give small and medium-sized businesses the same technology advantages that large enterprises take for granted — clean data systems, AI-powered automation, and blockchain capabilities where they create real value. We build practical solutions, not proofs of concept.

What We're Building

YieldLife

A DeFi analytics platform purpose-built for the Cardano ecosystem. YieldLife captures daily APY snapshots across lending markets and liquidity pools — giving users a full historical view of yield performance, not just today's rates. Built on a Python/Flask backend with PostgreSQL, it demonstrates exactly how clean data infrastructure and blockchain integration produce products users can trust. As Midnight (the Cardano privacy sidechain) matures, YieldLife is positioned to incorporate privacy-preserving portfolio views — letting users analyze their positions without exposing wallet data on-chain.
